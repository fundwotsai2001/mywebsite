Hello! I’m Fang Duo Tsai (蔡芳鐸), a master's student from the Music and AI Lab at National Taiwan University, advised by the wonderful Prof. Yi-Hsuan Yang. I am working on Controllable Music Generation with Shih-Lun Wu as my mentor, and we published MuseControlLite to ICML 2025 and AP-adapter to ISMIR 2024.

Prior to joining the Music and AI Lab, I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University. During my undergraduate studies, I worked with Prof. Yu-Chiang Wang on text-to-image and Prof. Hung-Yun Hsieh on WIFI motion detection. Additionally, I interned at Realtek, working on audio encoding.
